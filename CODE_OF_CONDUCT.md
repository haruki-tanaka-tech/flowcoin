# Code of Conduct

## Scope

The FlowCoin project — this repository, its issues, pull requests,
discussions, and the `t.me/flowcoin_main` Telegram channel.

## Principles

1. **Attack ideas, not people.** Code review finds bugs. Personal
   attacks find nothing.
2. **Consensus is forever.** Opinions about consensus-breaking
   changes are welcome; campaigning to ram them through is not.
3. **No crypto-shilling.** This is a code project, not an investment
   community. Price talk, pump-coordination, and "when moon" belong
   elsewhere.
4. **No harassment.** Whether targeted at contributors, reviewers,
   maintainers, or anyone else. Doxxing, sustained hostility, sexual
   harassment, and threats result in a permanent ban.
5. **Credit and attribution.** Cite sources. Don't misrepresent others'
   work as your own.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may at their discretion:
- Remove comments / commits / issues that violate these principles.
- Lock threads that have lost their way.
- Temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Appeals go to the maintainer directly
(`pilatovichkristian2@gmail.com`). Appeals should explain what
happened, why the action was unfair, and what you'd do differently.

## Attribution

Heavily inspired by the Bitcoin Core and Monero codes of conduct,
pared down to essentials.
